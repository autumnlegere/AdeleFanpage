About Adele (Bio)

Adele is a pop soul singer who has been topping the charts in music since 2008. She was born in London, England in 1988.
She has a following of loyal fans who call themselves "daydreamers" after a song on her debut album.
Adele is known for her unique, soulful voice and her ability to tell stories through her song lyrics.
